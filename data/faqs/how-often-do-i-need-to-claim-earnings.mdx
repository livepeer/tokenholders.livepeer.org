  question: "How often do I need to claim my LPT tokens?",
  category: 'staking'
}

Earnings are automatically claimed anytime you stake, unstake, or restake. You only ever have to manually claim earnings if it's been over 100 rounds since you last performed any of these actions in which case the Livepeer Explorer app will prompt you to manually claim your earnings. Learn more about claiming earnings [here](https://forum.livepeer.org/t/difference-between-reward-and-claimearnings/244).
